Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Coloring Book ("the App") handles information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

The App is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information necessary to provide the service:

  • Image Generation Prompts: When you create a coloring page, the text prompt you enter is sent to our server to generate the image. These prompts are not stored or associated with any personal identifier.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, address)
  • Account or login information
  • Device identifiers
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Cookies or similar technologies

How We Use Information

The text prompts you submit are used solely to generate coloring page images through our AI image generation service. The generated images are stored on our server to display in the app's gallery.

Third-Party Services

The App uses OpenAI's image generation API to create coloring pages. When you request an image, your prompt is processed by OpenAI's services. Please refer to OpenAI's Privacy Policy for information about how they handle data.

Data Storage

Generated images are stored on our server. The App does not store any personal data on your device beyond the standard app cache managed by iOS.

Children's Privacy

The App is designed to be family-friendly and suitable for children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The App does not require registration or personal information to use.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.
